Pre-Launch

Beacon is in its founding phase. Our Form 1023 is filed with the IRS and we expect a determination in the coming weeks. Governance documents are adopted and seed funding is in place. We are not yet accepting formal applications, but we are actively in conversation with prospective projects.

If you're working on a GCR project and want to be notified when applications open, or if you'd like to discuss whether Beacon might be a good fit for your work in the future, reach out via our contact form.

What to expect when applications open

Beacon will keep the application process lean. We want to understand your project well enough to assess fit, not front-load bureaucracy before we've established whether we can help.

We anticipate asking for:

  • Project name and lead contact
  • Brief description of the project
  • GCR relevance statement (1–2 paragraphs)
  • Requested services and estimated budget
  • Anticipated timeline

In the meantime, you can check whether Beacon is likely to be a good fit for your project, or read about our scope and focus areas.
