What we do

Beacon enables the best possible research into catastrophic and existential risk. We provide fiscal sponsorship, administrative and management overhead, and compliance infrastructure so researchers can focus on the work that matters.

We are relentlessly focused on catastrophic and existential risk, with a deep understanding of the funding and research ecosystem around it. We know what researchers and project directors need, and we want to give it to them and get out of the way.

Why Beacon

Researchers and project founders working on catastrophic risk face a choice: spend months incorporating a nonprofit, operate under a fiscal sponsor that doesn't understand the domain, or sacrifice independence to work within established institutions. The results are opportunity cost, preventable delays, and bottlenecks on getting good research done.

Beacon exists to close that gap. We combine fiscal sponsorship with deep GCR domain focus. When a project operates under Beacon, funders know that someone who understands the field has vetted it, and project leads get infrastructure that anticipates their needs rather than getting in their way.

Beacon's model works on three levels: quality filtering that signals GCR credibility to funders, operational infrastructure delivered by people who understand the domain, and network effects from housing multiple GCR projects under one roof. The result is that good projects launch faster and funders can deploy capital with more confidence.
