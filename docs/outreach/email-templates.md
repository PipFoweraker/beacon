## General sending guidelines

1. **Pip sends all emails personally.** These are relationship emails, not marketing.
2. **Personalize every email.** The templates are scaffolding. A template that reads like a template defeats the purpose.
3. **One ask per email.** Don't combine "refer projects to us" with "also would you fund us."
4. **Follow up once.** If no response after 1-2 weeks, one brief follow-up. Then stop.
5. **Track everything in `tracker.md`.** Date sent, response status, follow-up date.
