Resources

Practical guidance for starting, funding, and managing GCR projects.

These resources are for anyone working on global catastrophic risk — whether or not you're seeking fiscal sponsorship from Beacon.

  • Funding & Grants Who funds GCR work, how to write applications, budget templates, and active opportunities.
  • Project Management Success patterns, common failure modes, and templates for GCR projects.
  • Compliance & Governance Nonprofit compliance basics, financial reporting, conflict of interest, and record-keeping.
