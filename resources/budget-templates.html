Budget Templates

Full breakdown of the default assumptions for small, medium, and large GCR projects. All figures below are used as starting points in the Budget Builder and can be adjusted freely.

These templates are based on observed patterns from GCR/EA sector projects, Form 990 filings, and 80,000 Hours job board data. They represent typical costs for a 12-month project. Your project will differ, and that's expected. The templates exist to make sure you consider each category, not to prescribe what your budget should be.

All figures are generated from the same data that powers the Budget Builder. If Beacon updates its reference data, these tables update automatically.
